FAQ & Objections
Your questions, answered

Common objections — handled honestly.

The eight questions every café owner asks before switching. Real answers, no marketing fluff.

I already use Square / Loyverse. Is switching worth it?
If margin tracking, recipe costs, or AI insights matter to you — yes. Sizzle imports your sales data and waives your first month. You can keep both running in parallel for a week to compare. Most owners decide in 3 days.
Will it work for my karinderya?
Yes. Karinderya is one of the 7 verticals we ship with pre-configured menus and dashboards. You don't need a POS terminal — log sales by total in 2 taps from your phone. Free tier covers it entirely.
I'm not techy. Can I actually use this?
If you can use Messenger and GCash, you can use Sizzle. 60-second signup, in-app video tutorials, and the founder DMs you personally if you get stuck. No accountant or IT person required.
What happens to my data if I cancel?
Your data is yours. Cancel anytime, downgrade to Free and keep using the core dashboard, or request a full CSV export and we email it to you within 24 hours. No hostage situations.
What if Sizzle goes down during a rush?
Sizzle runs on Vercel and Supabase — the same infrastructure powering thousands of production apps. 99.9% uptime target. Status page at status.sizzle.app. POS is offline-tolerant and syncs when reconnected.
Can my staff use it without seeing my financials?
Yes. Owner and staff roles are built in. Staff sees POS, menu, and shifts — not reports, analytics, or payroll. One account, multiple users, clear permissions.
Will the ₱399 price go up later?
Maybe — Sizzle is in early-stage pricing. The first 100 cafés get ₱399 locked in for the life of their subscription, even if we raise prices for new signups. Sign up early to lock yours.
Do I need a business permit / BIR registration?
No. Sizzle works the same whether you're a registered BIR business or running an informal kainan. VAT receipts are available when you turn them on in settings; otherwise receipts print without VAT lines. You decide.
