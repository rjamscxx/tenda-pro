Industries
Built for these businesses

Seven industries. One operating system.

Sizzle ships pre-configured for the seven most common Filipino food businesses. Each comes with a starter menu, ingredient list, supplier list, and dashboard layout tuned for that vertical.

☕
Café & Coffee Shop
Espresso · Pandesal · Pastry
Track milk & bean cost-per-shot, monitor barista shifts, and forecast Saturday volume before payroll. Public QR menu for table ordering.
Critical features: Recipe costs, low-stock alerts, shift scheduling, AI cost-per-cup insights.
🍕
Pizzeria
Wood-fired · Delivery-first
Higher ingredient consumption per ticket means stock alerts fire more often — Sizzle's auto-deplete keeps you ahead. Multi-channel POS for dine-in and delivery riders.
Critical features: Channel-split sales, delivery margin tracking, peak-hour staff scheduling.
🍚
Karinderya
Turo-turo · Owner-operated
Designed for one-person operations. Quick-log sales without a POS, daily revenue + waste tracking, and AI tips written in plain Tagalog about which adobo runs ran short.
Critical features: Manual total entry, waste log, Tagalog AI insights, free forever tier.
🧋
Boba & Milk Tea
Topping-heavy · Mall kiosk
Per-cup tapioca / topping cost tracked at the ingredient level. Group orders (Grab/Foodpanda batches) logged in one tap. Multi-location for franchise owners.
Critical features: Recipe items per cup, channel breakdown, multi-venue (Pro), supplier links.
🍔
Burger Joint
Combo meals · QSR
Combo-meal margins broken down per component. VAT-aware receipts for BIR-registered branches. Forecast meat thaw based on next week's projected volume.
Critical features: Recipe items per dish, food cost %, VAT receipts, 7-day forecast (Pro).
🥐
Bakery / Panaderia
Flour-heavy · Batch production
Yield-based recipes (one batch = N pieces) with per-piece cost. Morning shift attendance feeds 6 AM payroll automatically. Catch waste from unsold afternoon trays.
Critical features: Recipe yields, shifts & payroll, waste log (Pro), low-stock alerts.
🍜
Ramen / Noodle Shop
Slow-prep · Premium-priced
Broth-prep batched the night before; Sizzle tracks consumption and forecasts batch sizes. Premium-ticket margin reporting reveals which bowls justify the slow cook.
Critical features: Recipe batch yields, menu engineering (Pro), per-dish margins, daily digest.
＋
Any other food business
Custom setup in 60s
Food trucks, cloud kitchens, dessert shops, juice bars — Sizzle works for any small F&B operation. Build your menu, set your costs, you're live.
Common requests: Onboarding support is direct from the founder — DM us on signup.
